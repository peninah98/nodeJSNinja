Bible scriptures

Genesis

Chapter 1

In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.

God said, "Let there be light," and there was light.

God said, "Let there be darkness," and there was darkness.

God said, "Let the light shine upon the earth," and the light shone.

God called the light "day," and the darkness he called "night."

God made a firmament, and let it be the sky.

God made the firmament black, and let it be the ground.

God made the firmament white, and let it be the firmament.

God made the firmament blue, and let it be the firmament.

God said, "Let the waters be gathered together into one place, and let the dry land appear."

God gathered the waters, and let them become a single place, and the dry land appeared.

God said, "Let the waters be called the seas, and let the dry land be called the land of the living."

God made the seas of water, and let them be the waters.

God made the dry land of the living, and let it be the land.

God said, "Let the earth sprout vegetation, so that the man may eat bread from it."

God made the earth sprout vegetation, and let it be the earth.

God said, "Let the man sow his seed in the earth, and let it sprout."
